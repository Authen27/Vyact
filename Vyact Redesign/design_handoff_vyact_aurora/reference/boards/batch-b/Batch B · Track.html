M9 · End / delete semantics. Ending defaults to going forward — the schedule stops (RRULE UNTIL = today), history untouched. Pause skips without deleting; full deletion is explicit about what it removes and sits last. Cancelling a split follows the same pattern: future shares only, settled history stays.
